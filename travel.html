Delhi,India

India's capital, is a vast metropolis in Northern India, historically significant as the seat of empires, blending ancient monuments like the Red Fort and Humayun's Tomb with modern vibrancy, featuring Old Delhi's bazaars and New Delhi's planned avenues, situated on the Yamuna River, bordered by Haryana and Uttar Pradesh, and a major cultural, political, and economic hub.

Sydney,Australia

Sydney is Australia's largest city, the capital of New South Wales, famous for its stunning harbour, iconic landmarks like the Opera House and Harbour Bridge, beautiful beaches (Bondi), vibrant culture, and a blend of city life with access to nature, serving as a major global hub.
